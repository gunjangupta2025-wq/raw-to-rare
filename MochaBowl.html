Vegan Mocha Yoghurt Bowl

The Fusion of Caffeine & Cream

Prep Time: 5 mins

Caffeine Level: Moderate

Hardness: Beginner (emulsion focus)

The Components

  • 1.5 cups Coconut or Almond Yoghurt
  • 1 shot Cold Espresso (or 1 tsp Instant Coffee)
  • 1 tbsp Raw Cacao Powder
  • 1 tsp Maple Syrup (optional)
  • Toppings: Shaved dark chocolate & Hazelnuts

The Transformation

  1. The Infusion: Sift the raw cacao powder into the coffee shot. Stir until you have a thick, grit-free mocha "slurry."
  2. The Folding: Slowly add the mocha mixture into the white yoghurt. For a "Rare" look, stop stirring early to leave dark espresso streaks.
  3. The Balance: Taste the bitterness. If the cacao is too dominant, add maple syrup drop by drop to stabilize the acidity.
  4. The Finish: Top with crushed hazelnuts to provide a structural crunch against the soft mocha base.

Technical Notes

"The interaction between the healthy fats in the coconut yoghurt and the polyphenols in the raw cacao creates a slow-release energy profile."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Serve in a wide, shallow stone bowl. Use a microplane to shave dark chocolate over the top in a fine, uniform dust. Place three hazelnuts in a linear arrangement to emphasize the "Rare" geometric design.
