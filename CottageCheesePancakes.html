Cottage Cheese Pancakes

Engineering Protein-Dense Fluffiness

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Hardness: Intermediate (flip timing)

The Components

  • 1 cup Cottage Cheese (small curd)
  • 2 Large Organic Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats (blended into flour)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract & 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • A pinch of Baking Powder

The Transformation

  1. The Base: Pulse the cottage cheese, eggs, and vanilla in a blender. This breaks down the curds into a smooth, liquid emulsion.
  2. The Integration: Add the oat flour and baking powder. Let the batter rest for 5 minutes to allow the oats to hydrate, ensuring a consistent density.
  3. The Heat: Pour small circles onto a medium-heat pan. The cottage cheese creates a delicate structure that requires a low and slow sear.
  4. The Flip: Wait for small bubbles to form on the surface. Flip gently to preserve the rare, airy center.

Technical Notes

"The high moisture content of cottage cheese acts as a natural steam agent, lifting the pancakes from within while the oats provide the necessary structural bridge."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Stack four pancakes in a precise tower. Garnish with a linear row of fresh blueberries and a light dusting of cinnamon to mirror the sophisticated, engineered look of your kitchen masterpieces.
