Baked Avocado Eggs

The Art of the Perfect Transformation

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Hardness: Intermediate (requires yolk stability)

The Components

  • 2 Large, ripe Hass Avocados
  • 4 Fresh Organic Eggs
  • Chives, finely minced
  • Red pepper flakes & Sea salt

The Transformation

  1. The Prep: Halve the avocados and remove the pits. Scoop out about 1 tablespoon of flesh to create a deeper "well" for the egg.
  2. The Stability: Place avocados in a baking dish, using foil "rings" to keep them from tipping.
  3. The Sear: Crack one egg into each hole. Season immediately with salt and pepper.
  4. The Heat: Bake at 200°C for 12–15 minutes until the whites are set but yolks remain rare.

Technical Notes

"The key to the 'Rare' center is temperature control. If the avocado is too cold, the egg won't cook evenly."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Serve on a matte black slate to contrast the vibrant green. Garnish with a linear sprinkle of chives across the center for a professional, engineered look.
