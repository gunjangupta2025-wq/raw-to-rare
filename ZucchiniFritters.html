Healthy Zucchini Fritters

The Engineering of the Perfect Crunch

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Hardness: Intermediate (moisture control)

The Components

  • 2 Medium Zucchinis (grated)
  • 2 Organic Eggs (lightly beaten)
  • 1/2 cup Chickpea Flour (for a gluten-free bond)
  • 2 Scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder & Sea Salt

The Transformation

  1. The Extraction: Place grated zucchini in a clean towel and squeeze forcefully. Removing 90% of the raw liquid is the only way to avoid a soggy result.
  2. The Binding: Mix the dry zucchini with eggs, flour, and spices. The batter should be thick and hold its shape.
  3. The Sear: Heat a lightly oiled pan. Drop spoonfuls of batter and flatten slightly. Cook until the edges turn a deep, rigid gold.
  4. The Flip: Flip only once. This maintains the structural integrity of the rare, tender center.

Technical Notes

"The science here is simple: water is the enemy of the crunch. Chickpea flour acts as a superior structural bridge compared to standard wheat flour."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Stack three fritters vertically to create height. Side-plate with a dollop of cold lemon-yogurt sauce and garnish with fresh dill to provide a bright, herbal contrast to the savory sear.
