Vegan Cinnamon Roll Pancakes

The Art of the Plant-Based Spiral

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 12 mins

Hardness: Advanced (precision swirling)

The Components

  • 1.5 cups Gluten-Free Flour Blend
  • 1 cup Almond Milk + 1 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar (Vegan Buttermilk)
  • The Swirl: Melted Coconut Oil, Brown Sugar, Cinnamon
  • The Glaze: Powdered Sugar & splash of Almond Milk

The Transformation

  1. The Chemical Reaction: Mix the almond milk and vinegar. Let sit for 5 mins to curdle; this provides the "rare" lift needed for fluffy vegan cakes.
  2. The Batter: Combine wet and dry ingredients. The batter must be viscous enough to support the weight of the cinnamon swirl.
  3. The Spiral: Once the pancake is on the griddle, pipe the cinnamon mixture in a precise outward spiral.
  4. The Flip: Flip carefully. The sugar in the swirl will caramelize instantly upon contact with the heat.

Technical Notes

"The 'Vegan Buttermilk' is a functional necessity. The acid reacts with the leavening agents to create internal air pockets that mimic an egg-based structure."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Drizzle the white glaze in a zigzag pattern that intersects the cinnamon spirals. Serve on a white ceramic plate to emphasize the contrast between the dark cinnamon and the light glaze.
