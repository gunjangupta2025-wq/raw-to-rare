Breakfast Berry Parfait

The Geometry of Layered Freshness

Prep Time: 5 mins

Chill Time: None

Hardness: Beginner (focus on layering)

The Components

  • 1 cup Greek Yogurt (Plain or Vanilla)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Blueberries & Strawberries
  • 1/4 cup Honey-Toasted Granola
  • 1 tsp Organic Honey or Agave
  • Fresh Mint for garnish

The Transformation

  1. The Base: Spoon 1/3 of the yogurt into the bottom of a clear glass to create a stable foundation.
  2. The Berry Layer: Add a dense layer of mixed berries. Press them slightly against the glass for visual impact.
  3. The Crunch: Sprinkle a thin layer of granola to provide a structural barrier between yogurt layers.
  4. The Repeat: Repeat the layers until the glass is full, finishing with a yogurt peak.

Technical Notes

"To prevent the granola from losing its 'Rare' crunch, assemble this masterpiece immediately before serving."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Use a tall, tapered glass to show off the clean horizontal lines of the layers. Drizzle honey in a circular motion and top with a single mint leaf for a pop of contrasting color.
