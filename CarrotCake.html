Healthier Carrot Cake

The Geometry of Sweetness

Prep Time: 20 mins

Bake Time: 35 mins

Hardness: Advanced (texture control)

The Components

  • 2 cups Raw Carrots, finely grated
  • 1.5 cups Whole Wheat Flour
  • 2 Fresh Eggs (room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt (for moisture)
  • Cinnamon, Nutmeg & Ginger

The Transformation

  1. The Grate: Use the smallest setting on your grater for the carrots. Finely grated carrots melt into the batter during the sear.
  2. The Emulsion: Whisk eggs and yogurt until perfectly smooth. This creates the structural "bridge" for the cake.
  3. The Integration: Fold dry ingredients into the wet slowly. Over-mixing ruins the "rare" soft crumb.
  4. The Bake: Bake at 180°C until a tester comes out clean. Let rest in the pan to retain internal humidity.

Technical Notes

"The science of this cake relies on the carrots' water content. Grating them fresh ensures the cake stays moist without excessive oil."

The Final Plate (Plating)

Serve a vertical slice to showcase the orange flecks against the wheat crumb. Top with a thin, architectural layer of Greek yogurt frosting and a single walnut half for a professional finish.
